Visit Bhaktapur

Enjoy culture and nature in one of the most culture rich district of Nepal.

Top three activities to do at Bhaktapur

Attend various festivals and events

Bhaktapur has various cultural festivals and events happening throughout the year.

Get into the nature

Bhaktapur has peaceful and beautiful places like Nagarkot, Gundu, Ghyampe Danda where you can take short trekking.

Explore the city

Bhaktapur has little shops for local foods, Thanka paintings, cloths, etc throughout the city.
